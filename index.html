Alices Adventures In Wonderland

About Alice in Wonderland

Alice in Wonderland is the story of a little girl who falls down a rabbit hole and ends up in a whole new world. As she tries to escape back home she meets many, many characters and makes friends with most of them.

The original illustrations of various Alice characters.

About this website.

On this site my goal is to have a page for each Alice character from the original story. I will reference how they appeared in other media but my main focus is Lewis Carroll's original work.
